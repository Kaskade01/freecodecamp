Donald J. Trump

45th President of the United States

Donald John Trump is the 45th President of the United States, in office since January 20, 2017. In addition to being a politician, he is a successful business magnate and television personality as well. The son of a wealthy real estate developer, Trump attended the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania before taking charge of his family’s business. An astute businessman and charismatic leader, he built and renovated numerous hotels, casinos, and office towers during his business career, accumulating a net worth of billions. He also owned several beauty pageants and ventured into reality television as well. Expanding the horizons of his ambitions, he entered national politics in the early 2000s and set his eyes on the presidential office. As a politician, his career was marred by allegations of sexual misconduct and he also earned much criticism for his extravagant lifestyle and controversial comments on immigrants from Islamic nations. Despite the controversies surrounding him, Trump ran for the 2016 presidential election as a Republican and defeated Democratic candidate Hillary Clinton in a surprise victory. Donald Trump is the first person to assume presidency without prior military or governmental service and at 70, he is also the oldest one to assume the office.

  • 1968 - Graduates from University of Pennsylvania.
  • 1972 - Becomes owner of The Trump Organization.
  • 1972 - Marries Ivana Zelníčková.
  • 1977 - Ivana gives birth to Donald Trump Jr.
  • 1981 - Ivana gives birth to Ivanka Trump.
  • 1983 - Grand opening of Trump Tower in Manhattan.
  • 1984 - Ivana gives birth to Eric Trump.
  • 1987 - "Trump: The Art of the Deal" is published.
  • 1990 - Trump faces bankruptcy.
  • 1992 - Ivana and Trump divorce.
  • 1993 - Marries Marla Maples.
  • 1993 - Marla gives birth to Tiffany Trump.
  • 1995 - Trump Hotels and Casino Resorts goes public.
  • 1999 - Maples and Trump divorce.
  • 1999 - Trump's father, Fred Trump, passes away.
  • 1999 - Trump founds Trump Model Management.
  • 2004 - Hosts NBC's "The Apprentice."
  • 2005 - Marries Melania Knauss.
  • 2006 - Melania gives birth to Barron Trump.
  • 2009 - Grand openiong of Trump International Hotel and Tower in Chicago.
  • 2015 - Announces candidacy for President of the United States.
  • 2016 - Trump wins the U.S. presidential election.
